# AI-Assisted Oracle Reporting & Business Decision Support System

## Project Overview

This project is a Python, SQL, and Streamlit-based reporting application that converts business datasets into KPI dashboards, automated summaries, and decision-support insights.

It is designed as a portfolio project for Oracle Package Specialist, Business Technology Consultant, Reporting Analyst, and Enterprise Application Support roles.

## Business Problem

Business teams often depend on manual spreadsheet analysis to understand KPI movement, reporting exceptions, and operational trends. This slows down decision-making and creates inconsistent reporting narratives.

## Solution

This application allows users to upload business reporting data, view KPI summaries, identify SLA issues, and generate stakeholder-friendly reporting summaries.

## Key Features

- Upload CSV business datasets
- Generate KPI summary metrics
- Detect SLA breaches and high-priority items
- Produce executive-style reporting summaries
- Prepare dashboard-ready data
- Demonstrate AI-assisted reporting structure
