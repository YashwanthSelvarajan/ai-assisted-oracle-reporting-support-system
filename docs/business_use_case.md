# Business Use Case

## Objective
Create a decision-support reporting system that helps business users understand operational performance, SLA risks, and automation opportunities.

## Users
- Business Analysts
- Reporting Analysts
- Oracle Package Support Teams
- Functional Consultants
- Operations Managers

## Business Value
- Converts raw reporting data into KPI summaries
- Identifies SLA-breached items
- Supports stakeholder-ready executive summaries
- Reduces manual reporting effort
- Provides a foundation for AI-assisted business reporting
